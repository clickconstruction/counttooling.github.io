Custom Icon Tips

What makes a great icon for upload:

  • Simple shapes — Icons are shown small; clear outlines work best
  • Single color — Your icon is tinted by the counter color
  • Path-based SVG — Use path, rect, circle, ellipse, or line elements
  • Avoid transforms — Rotate or scale in your editor before export
  • Square viewBox — e.g. 0 0 24 24 or 0 0 64 64
