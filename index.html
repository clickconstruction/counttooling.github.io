Frequently asked questions

Do I need to install anything?

No. CountTooling runs in any modern web browser — there's nothing to download or update. You can optionally install it as an app for a home-screen icon.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Once a takeoff is loaded you can keep counting, measuring, and marking up with no connection. It auto-saves with local backups and syncs back up when you reconnect.

What trades is it for?

Construction and plumbing estimating — counting fixtures and devices, and measuring linear runs like pipe and conduit, right on the plan PDF.

Can I export to my pricing software?

Yes. Copy a takeoff straight into PipeTooling, copy a tab-delimited summary for email, or export a marked-up PDF with the takeoff report included.

Does it work on a tablet?

Yes. It's touch-friendly — single-finger pan, pinch to zoom, long-press for the menu — so you can mark up plans on a tablet in the field.

How is it different from desktop estimating software?

It runs in the browser with nothing to install, works on any device and even offline, and exports into your pricing workflow — versus heavy Windows-only desktop tools.
